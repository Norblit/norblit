About me:

I was born and raised in Queens, New York. I grew up with computers practically all my life, learning how to troubleshoot, repair, and even break them apart. I've always had a curious mind for technology and have ever since grown excited for what the future may have in store. I got my first taste of site-building back in 2013 on a site I called everythingyoucouldeverwant.yolasite.com and it was almost natural how quickly I picked things up. From embedding youtube videos to designing layouts and even playing with the font text or sizes. From time to time, I would go back to fiddling on the web, inconsistently re-learning HTML/CSS until mid 2019. Since then, I decided to pursue my degree in Computer Programming and Software Development at Laguardia Community College narrowing my focus towards Web Development a semester later. I'm persistent, confident, and most importantly excited to be involved in this rapidly-growing industry, I look forward to working with you!
